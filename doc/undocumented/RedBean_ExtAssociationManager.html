extAssociate (line 5020)

Associates two beans with eachother. This method connects two beans with eachother, just

like the other associate() method in the Association Manager. The difference is however that this method accepts a base bean, this bean will be used as the basis of the association record in the link table. You can thus add additional properties and even foreign keys.
